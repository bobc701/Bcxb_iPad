Fielders are rated at each of their positions
as follows:

   6 — Gold glove quality at the position
   5 — All Star quality at the position
   4 — Above average among starters at the position
   3 — Average among starters at the position
   2 — Below average among starters at the position
   1 — Major defensive liability at the position
   0 or not rated — Not qualified to play the position
